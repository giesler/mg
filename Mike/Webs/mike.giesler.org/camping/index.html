Camping is probably my favorite hobby. I try to go whenever I get a chance, of course mainly during the summer months. I have gone all over out West. And I've done a lot of camping around Wisconsin. This page has some of my camping memories - pictures and stories - from some of my favorite camping trips [or that I happened to bring a camera along].

I usually go 'car-camping' where we pull out the tents but use the car to store most everything.  I also really like backpacking, but don't have the equipment to do it now.

Spring Break 1996

Rather then going to Florida, California, or something, I spent my first college spring break camping in southern Missouri.  Five of us decided to take our own trip.  It was really cold the first day, but from then on it was pretty nice weather.  And who can object to a week-long spring break that cost each of us only $50?
