Instead of the usual Christmas we celebrate every year, including gift exchanges and stuff, we decided to go as a family to California. On Christmas Day eight of us flew out to spend a week with Paula. It was a great trip - great break from the Wisconsin weather, though it wasn't 'hot' or anything, and it was the first time our family got everyone together since Paula's wedding. Here are some of the pictures and a little about our trip.

 

(12/25/96) We got to California and drove to the cliffs on the ocean. Rick almost fell in, unfortunately no one got that on camera. But we couldn't spend long there, we had to get to Paula's to eat - turkey dinner, her first turkey (a 28 pounder). And the whole meal turned out great.
